Support rights

The rights and entitlements of users or organizations to receive assistance and services related to VPN usage.

Description

Support rights in the context of VPN use cases refer to the entitlements that users or organizations have regarding technical assistance and resources related to their VPN services. This concept encompasses the availability of helpdesk support, troubleshooting, and guidance on VPN configuration and usage. Organizations that deploy VPNs often ensure that their employees have access to support channels to resolve issues that may arise during VPN connection, performance, or security. Support rights may also include access to documentation, user manuals, and training resources to help users understand how to effectively utilize the VPN service. Additionally, support rights can vary based on the type of VPN service contract, where premium plans might offer 24/7 support, while basic plans might provide limited assistance during business hours. Overall, understanding support rights is crucial for organizations to ensure that users can effectively leverage VPN technologies for secure remote access and data protection.

Examples

  • A company provides 24/7 technical support for its employees using a corporate VPN.
  • An individual user has access to online resources and FAQs for troubleshooting their VPN connection.

Additional Information

  • Support rights can include live chat, email support, and phone support options.
  • Organizations should define their support rights in their VPN usage policies to set clear expectations.
