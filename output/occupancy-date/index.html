Occupancy date

The date on which a user begins utilizing a VPN service for secure access.

Description

In the context of VPN use cases, the occupancy date refers to the specific date when a user starts using a Virtual Private Network (VPN) service to connect to the internet or a private network securely. This date is significant as it marks the commencement of the user's engagement with the VPN service, which is often linked to the user's subscription or trial period. The occupancy date can be critical for tracking usage patterns, managing billing cycles, and evaluating the effectiveness of the VPN service in meeting the user's needs for privacy and security. For businesses, the occupancy date can also help in understanding when employees begin utilizing the VPN, which can be essential for compliance and monitoring purposes. By analyzing occupancy dates across users, organizations can identify trends in VPN adoption and usage, assess the impact of remote work policies, and ensure that resources are adequately allocated to support secure connections.

Examples

  • A remote employee starts using a VPN on January 15, 2023, for secure access to company resources.
  • A new customer begins a VPN trial on March 1, 2023, to evaluate the service for personal privacy.

Additional Information

  • The occupancy date may coincide with the start of a subscription plan.
  • Monitoring occupancy dates can help in optimizing VPN infrastructure and support.
