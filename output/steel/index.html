Steel

Steel refers to a secure and robust VPN solution designed for high-performance and reliability.

Description

In the context of VPN use cases, 'Steel' signifies a type of virtual private network that emphasizes strong security measures, high throughput, and low latency. This term is often associated with VPN solutions that cater to businesses requiring secure remote access for their employees, particularly those working with sensitive data or in regulated industries. Steel VPNs utilize advanced encryption protocols to ensure data privacy and integrity while providing a seamless user experience. These solutions are crucial for organizations that operate across multiple geographic locations and need to maintain secure communications. In addition to encryption, Steel VPNs may incorporate features such as split tunneling, which allows users to access public internet resources while maintaining a secure connection to the corporate network. By leveraging Steel VPN technology, businesses can protect their digital assets, comply with data protection regulations, and enhance their overall cybersecurity posture.

Examples

  • A multinational corporation using Steel VPN to secure communications between offices in different countries.
  • Remote employees accessing company resources securely through a Steel VPN while working from home.

Additional Information

  • Steel VPNs often support various devices, including desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.
  • They may include features for user authentication, logging, and monitoring for enhanced security.
