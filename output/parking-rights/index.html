Parking rights

The ability of a user or organization to reserve or maintain exclusive access to a specific IP address or domain name via a VPN.

Description

Parking rights in the context of VPN use cases refer to the privileges associated with maintaining control over specific IP addresses or domain names that are associated with a VPN service. When a user or organization establishes parking rights, they can ensure that their selected IP addresses are reserved for their exclusive use, preventing others from accessing them. This is particularly important for businesses that require consistent and stable IP addresses for their operations, such as for remote access, secure communications, or to maintain their online reputation. Additionally, parking rights can help organizations manage their digital identities and security, as they can avoid the risk of IP addresses being used by malicious actors. This concept is especially relevant for companies that operate across different geographical locations and need to ensure compliance with local regulations or to maintain a specific online presence. Overall, parking rights enhance the flexibility and security of VPN usage for organizations and individuals alike.

Examples

  • A multinational company reserves specific IP addresses to ensure secure access for its remote employees.
  • An organization parks domain names with a VPN provider to maintain brand integrity and avoid phishing attacks.

Additional Information

  • Parking rights can vary in terms of duration and scope depending on the VPN provider.
  • Organizations must regularly review their parking rights to ensure they align with their evolving business needs.
