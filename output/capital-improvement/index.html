Capital improvement

Enhancements or upgrades to a VPN infrastructure to improve performance or security.

Description

In the context of VPN use cases, capital improvement refers to the strategic investments made to enhance the VPN infrastructure, ensuring it meets the growing demands for security, speed, and connectivity. This can include upgrading hardware, implementing advanced encryption protocols, or expanding bandwidth capabilities. Capital improvements are essential for organizations that rely on VPNs for remote access, secure communications, and data protection. By investing in capital improvements, businesses can address potential vulnerabilities, enhance user experience, and comply with regulatory requirements. These improvements can also facilitate the adoption of newer technologies such as cloud services and mobile devices, which require robust and secure network connections. As cybersecurity threats evolve, capital improvements ensure that the VPN infrastructure remains resilient against attacks and capable of supporting a distributed workforce effectively.

Examples

  • Upgrading to a next-generation firewall to enhance VPN security.
  • Increasing bandwidth capacity to support remote work and cloud applications.

Additional Information

  • Capital improvements can be part of a larger IT strategy focusing on digital transformation.
  • Regular assessments of VPN infrastructure can identify areas needing capital improvement.
