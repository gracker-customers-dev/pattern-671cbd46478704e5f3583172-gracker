Scheduled

Refers to pre-defined times for VPN connections to be established or terminated.

Description

In the context of VPN use cases, 'Scheduled' refers to the ability to configure VPN connections to automatically establish or disconnect at specific times. This feature is particularly useful for organizations that have predictable usage patterns or need to ensure secure connections only during certain hours of operation. For instance, a business may want its VPN to be active only during working hours to protect sensitive data when employees are accessing the network remotely. Scheduling can help in optimizing bandwidth usage and enhancing security by reducing the window for potential attacks when the VPN is inactive. Additionally, it can facilitate compliance with internal policies or regulations that require data protection measures during defined hours. This feature can be set up through VPN client software or network management systems, allowing administrators to easily manage when the VPN is operational.

Examples

  • A company schedules its VPN to run from 9 AM to 5 PM, ensuring secure access during work hours.
  • An educational institution sets its VPN to connect automatically for online classes held at specific times.

Additional Information

  • Scheduled connections can reduce costs by optimizing bandwidth usage.
  • Not all VPN services may support scheduling features, so it's important to check the capabilities of the chosen VPN solution.
